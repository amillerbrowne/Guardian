Team Running Safety

Boston University College of Engineering - ECE Senior Design 2016-17

Pictured left to right: Rosy Chen, Alexandra Miller-Browne, Christopher Considine, Inna Turshudzhyan, Nikita Gawande

Client: Isabel Pisano


Team Member Reason for choosing this project Future Plans
Alexandra Miller-Browne This senior design project stood out to me because as a runner myself, I am always very cautious of my surroundings and make sure that when I am on my route it is in a well lit familiar area and people are around. Having a device like Guardian I believe would truly be helpful to all runners to help them feel safe while completing their regular exercise routine. Working at GE in the Digital Technology Leadership Program
Nikita Gawande When I was researching the topics offered for Senior Design projects, I was horrified by the stories I read online of people going on a simple run and never coming home to their loved one again. This is an issue I knew that I could potentially solve and had to try if I could. Working as a Software Engineer for Optum, United Health
Rosy Chen What attracted me to this project was its ability to help people and save lives. Being a female and finding myself in strange situations occasionally, I definitely saw a need for a product like this. And the three incidences that happened in August (a little before we had to choose our project) also confirmed that. Working at Accenture as a Technology Analyst
Christopher Considine What attracted me to this project was two aspects that stood out from other projects. The first was the societal impact we can have with a product like this. As BU always encourages us to act as societal engineers, I thought this project could help me pursue that direction. The second thing that drew me to this project was the array of different technologies I will get to interface with. The project has so many components and has and will continue to expose me to many facets of computer engineering and design. Working as a Software Engineer for Optum, United Health
Inna Turshudzhyan Even though all of the Senior Design projects were really interesting, Running Safety was the only one that I would actually use myself. As somebody who lives in a big city, I am aware of all the danger surrounding me, especially young women. I realized that this device would help save lives, and that was the most important reason why I choose this project. Working as a Software Engineer at Microsoft
